our story

23 Years of Experience

Once upon a time, there was a group of enthusiastic adventurers who were passionate about exploring the world and experiencing all the different cultures it had to offer. They dreamed of sharing their love of travel with others and helping them to create unforgettable memories of their own.

With this in mind, they decided to start their own travel company, EagerExplorers, with the goal of providing personalized, immersive travel experiences that went beyond the typical tourist attractions. They wanted to take travelers off the beaten path and show them the hidden gems that only locals knew about.
